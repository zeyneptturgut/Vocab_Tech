Diary

[ dahy-uh-ree ]

Learn More:

Definition:

A diary is a daily record of personal experiences, thoughts, and reflections, typically kept for private use.

Example Sentence:

Every night before going to bed, Sarah writes in her diary about the events of the day.

Contextual Information:

Diaries are often used as a means of self-expression, self-reflection, or as a way to keep track of important events, feelings, or ideas over time. They can serve as valuable historical documents, providing insight into the daily lives and perspectives of individuals.
